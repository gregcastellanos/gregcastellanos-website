AI Consulting & Evaluation

Human centered AI literacy, evaluation, and responsible adoption.

Greg helps educators, small organizations, and creative teams understand AI, evaluate its output against clear criteria, and adopt it responsibly, drawing on two decades of assessment and instructional design.

Start a conversationView ventures

Format

Remote and Northern California. Workshops, evaluation support, and hands on advisory.

What this is

Practical AI guidance grounded in learning science, not hype. Greg applies rubric based evaluation, the same structured judgment he has used across thousands of learner and client interactions, to how AI tools are chosen, used, and reviewed.

Who it is for

Schools, educators, small businesses, nonprofits, and creative teams who want to use AI well and want a person who can explain it in plain language.

Problems Greg can help solve

A team unsure which AI tools to trust or how to check their output. An organization that wants an AI literacy workshop for staff or students. A workflow that could be clearer and faster with the right prompt and process design.

Focus

AI literacy for staff and studentsRubric based evaluation of AI outputResponsible adoption and guardrailsPrompt and process developmentAI supported workflow designCreative technology and tooling

Evidence

Assessment as the throughline.

Greg holds a Master of Arts in Teaching and has designed rubrics, assessments, and learning outcome measures across K to 12, college, adult, and community settings. He designs AI literacy programming for youth and is fluent across current tools including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Midjourney.
