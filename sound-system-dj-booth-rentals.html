Sound & DJ Booth Rentals

Custom sound systems and DJ booth support for gatherings that deserve care.

Greg builds and runs custom sound systems and DJ booths for weddings, retreats, sound healing sessions, corporate events, and community and private gatherings, with the technical setup and event support to match.

Availability

San Francisco Bay Area and Sonoma County. Travel considered for retreats and multi day events.

What this is

Sound and DJ support shaped around the room and the moment. Greg came to this work through Recreation Sound Systems, an audio hardware and event production venture, and brings a producer's attention to setup, sound, and flow.

Who it is for

Couples, hosts, retreat leaders, sound healing practitioners, community organizers, and companies who want thoughtful sound rather than a generic PA drop off.

Problems Greg can help solve

A wedding or retreat that needs warm, well placed sound and a calm technical hand. A gathering that needs a DJ booth and someone who can read a room. An event that needs both the gear and the person to run it.
