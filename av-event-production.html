AV & Event Production

Corporate AV and live event production in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Greg Castellanos is a live event and corporate AV professional with more than fifteen years supporting conferences, general sessions, executive presentations, and experiential events across Northern California.

Start a conversationView ventures

Availability

San Francisco Bay Area, Sonoma County, Marin County, and Northern California. Travel considered.

What this is

Hands-on production support and technical direction for shows where there is no margin for error. Greg operates graphics and playback, supports video engineering and V1 workflows, calls shows, and keeps the room calm from load in to strike.

Who it is for

Production companies, agencies, corporate teams, conference organizers, and venues that need a reliable operator or a production partner for a general session, keynote, panel, or multi day event.

Problems Greg can help solve

A show that needs a dependable graphics or playback operator. A general session that needs cue to cue calling. A production team that needs an extra set of experienced hands who can troubleshoot under pressure and communicate clearly with clients.

Capabilities

Graphics and playback operationV1 support and video engineeringTechnical direction and show callingProjection and LEDCamera and audio systemsRun of show and event logisticsPresentation and slide management

Evidence

Verified experience.

Greg manages 200 or more slide decks per show with live cue to cue calling. Through the production companies CAVL, JNSQ, Got Light, and Vario, he has supported productions for Google, Stripe, Atlassian, GitHub, Nasdaq, SF MoMA, and the World Economic Forum. These enterprise productions were delivered through those production companies rather than as direct client contracts.
