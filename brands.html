Clarity Before Style

Many branding problems are actually clarity problems. Greg helps identify what the work is really about, what needs to be said, what should be left out, and how the experience should feel when someone encounters it.

Connected Creative Direction

This work connects strategy, language, design, human experience, technology, and implementation. It can include naming, positioning, messaging, identity direction, websites, presentations, campaign concepts, launch support, and content systems.

For Real Projects

The work is especially useful when a founder, organization, initiative, place, or event has depth but has not yet found the words, structure, and visual direction that make it easy to understand.
