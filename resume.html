Summary

Twenty years across connected disciplines.

Gregory Castellanos is a founder, educator, and producer who designs systems that help people learn, adapt, and perform. His work spans corporate AV and live event production, learning and instructional design, creative and technical leadership, and human centered AI, backed by a Master of Arts in Teaching and two U.S. design patents.
