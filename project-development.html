Project Development

Turning ambitious and unusual ideas into actionable plans.

Greg helps founders and organizations move from a compelling but unshaped idea to a clear plan, connecting research, stakeholders, and place into something that can actually move forward.

Start a conversationView ventures

Format

Remote and Northern California. Planning, research synthesis, and development support.

What this is

Development work for projects that cross more than one discipline. Greg listens for the real opportunity, clarifies the vision, aligns the people and systems, and builds a plan that is usable rather than aspirational.

Who it is for

Founders, community projects, and organizations with a strong idea and no coherent plan, or with many disconnected initiatives that need to become one direction.

Problems Greg can help solve

An opportunity that is real but hard to explain. A property or place with potential and no direction. A venture that needs research synthesis, stakeholder alignment, and a workable first plan. Private client and property details are kept confidential.
