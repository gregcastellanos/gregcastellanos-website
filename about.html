About

A multidisciplinary career built around making complicated things workable.

Greg has worked across education, coaching, technology, events, entrepreneurship, strategy, and place. The fields change. The pattern stays consistent.

The throughline

This is not a random sequence of pivots. Greg sees the system, understands the people inside it, connects disciplines, and makes the next move practical.

Learning, technology, and making

Interactive multimedia, a Master of Arts in Teaching, STEAM education, maker programs, and AI literacy all point toward the same question: how do people learn, adapt, and make better decisions with better tools?

Sound, events, and operations

Patented audio technology and years of live production built technical fluency, calm under pressure, and a practical feel for how groups move through an experience.

People, place, and systems

Castellanos Coaching, consulting, creative direction, and Common Ground Works sit under one umbrella because they all involve people, environments, and decisions that need to become usable.
