What This Work Holds

Education, strategy, empathy, structure, accountability, creativity, and real-world practice. The goal is not to make someone sound impressive on paper. The goal is to help them understand what is happening, build useful systems, and practice the next step until it becomes more possible.

The Castellanos Compass

Within Castellanos Coaching, Greg uses the Castellanos Compass as a human-development framework with four stages: Clarity, Alignment, Foundation, and Expansion. On this umbrella site, the Compass is introduced as part of the coaching practice, not copied in full.
