Greg's role

Founder, inventor, producer, and technical director, 2013 to 2020

Context

An original sound, product invention, and event production venture, active 2013 to 2020.

What Greg did

Greg led industrial design, rapid prototyping, fabrication, and manufacturing, took product from concept to a shipped product, and directed branding, sales, and vendor relationships. He bridged hardware and live experiential events under one brand.

Outcome and scale

The work produced two U.S. design patents, a portable sound system and a portable yoga block speaker, and connected product design directly to event production.
