Privacy, in plain English.

A short, honest note about the limited information this site handles.

Overview

What this site collects, and why.

The contact form

When you use the contact form, it collects the information you enter — your name, email address, and message — so Greg can read and respond to your inquiry.

How your information is used

Your information is used only to respond to your inquiry. Greg does not sell visitor information, and it is not shared for advertising.

No advertising or behavioral tracking

This site does not use third-party advertising or behavioral analytics, and it does not set advertising or tracking cookies. Your light or dark theme preference is stored only in your own browser.

Form processing

Contact form submissions are handled by Web3Forms, a third-party form service, solely to pass your message to Greg at the public work email.

Questions about your information

You can contact Greg at any time about information you have submitted, at gregcastellanoswork@gmail.com.
