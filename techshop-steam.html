TechShop STEAM and Maker Education

A hands on STEAM curriculum and maker education program built around fabrication, technology, design, and practical problem solving.

Start a conversationView ventures

Greg's role

Curriculum designer, educator, and program developer, 2014 to 2019

Context

The flagship STEAM education program for a national makerspace company, 2014 to 2019.

What Greg did

Greg designed and launched the STEAM program, built project based summer camp and after school curriculum, and taught 2D and 3D design, Adobe Creative Suite, laser cutting, CNC, 3D printing, and electronics. He co designed TechShop Inside, a mobile makerspace serving underserved communities.

Outcome and scale

The curriculum was adopted across multiple TechShop locations nationwide, and Greg taught hundreds of learners across the program.
